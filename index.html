The real joy


In a persistently changing world of today, we all tend to run behind our interests and achieve our goals. We all learned, with time, to become more pragmatic and the subtler aspects of life were eventually sacrificed for the so called 'meaningful life'. This used to work fine until some time. But slowly and gradually, we realised that the instinct of craving for the 'goal' and the tedium of achieving it could give us nothing more than momentary happiness. Even after combining it with the incentives assured aftermath, one could easily feel the absence of something, which was very intimidating otherwise. This feeling of emptiness has haunted almost all of our minds at some point of time. Happiness is not an attribute of the goal - it associates itself with the process itself. That very process which is being neglected for the goal! It has created a vicious cycle wherein we are neglecting something which we want to have by us every moment! What an irony.

It has been the same story for most of the normal us, with our mind playing the conjuring with us, involving and affecting us with its insidious acts. And we could not master over this trickster yet. The mind is meant to be a slave and not a master. It has wonderful suggestions when churned for goodness, and at the same time, the most terrible thoughts when set free - like a horse without reigns. The wise men of the yore realised this fact very soon.

" AHAM BRAHMAASMI! "

It literally means - I am the creator.This small statement holds the true essence of the extra dimension of life that we hold within us- our mind. When expanded in the right manner, we can feel the power of this mind, that creates a new world inside us through creativity which manifests into reality in the form of our perceptions. Such is the power of this mind, that makes us the Brahma of the world within us. the thoughts that we think to be our are actually instances of manifestation of this world. Whether we suffer or enjoy, is all about how we perceive the outer world through this inner world. Hence said that happiness is a state of being and not the goal itself. In the pursuit of our goals in this fast changing world, we have, for our benefits, restricted this world to morbid boundaries. The creativity is exhausted almost completely to create something common! We are all trying to be a same type of person, a person whom we find ideal according to our needs. This has lead to a situation wherein the different expanding worlds are turning out to be the same, eventually losing their uniqueness - that uniqueness which is the elixir of life. That elixir which has led to the sustainance of this world. The real happiness lies in the jovial conjugation of this outer and inner world, a conjugation that demands no perfection, but is still perfect in itself. That's how this dimension proceeds.

Therefore, in order to find true happiness, we ought to shun the habit of looking for happiness in petty materials and belongings, and rather we should start enjoying the present state of our being, thanking the holy creator for almost all things - small or large, visible or invisible, perfect or imperfect that co-exist with us to create the perfect conjugation of the two imperfect worlds.And believe me, happiness found this way, would fill all the void in the life, and would drive us closer to the eternal imperfect truth, away from the gullible perfect illusion.

My Hostel


The topic to my essay might seem quite bland and tasteless to most of the readers. But, at this point of time, when I am experiencing the same phase in my life, I am finally able to pen my thoughts down. For a formal beginning I will try to define a hostel in the most naive and crude way. A hostel is a shelter for the students who come from far off places. Students live there with each other and learn the value of discipline and co-operation. The atmosphere of a hostel is conducive to study. Generally, hostels are situated close to school or college. Thus the teachers have direct watch and supervision over the students in the hostels.Further, it saves time of the students for study. The hostel life is a disciplined life. Hostels actually provide us something more than mere accommodation. They give us a new life, a type of life which we like and dislike at the same time. It is not easy to start a new life all alone with almost no aid by our side. At the same time, it gives us an exposure of the outer world and its ways of functioning. It also acts as a mirror to our inner self wherein we get a chance to understand who we actually are. For most of us, hostel life apparently seems quite fascinating and colourful, but only till we begin experiencing it. It changes our lives in a drastic way. It takes a few days for us to become a mature and aware adult from an oblivious and carefree child. The earlier days of hostels are not easy for anyone, but things go down well with the passage of time. There are a lot of feelings in the soft corner of our hearts, but we learn the art of concealing them and try to prepare ourselves of the world ahead. The earlier days are filled with nostalgia, because we are not able to adjust to the new environment, specially with the food and the accommodation. Food is the worst part and the most morbid aspect of our lives. Specially for pupils who have come from far places. Hostels can provide us with all the luxuries but they lack comfort. The best part part of our lives are our friends, partially because they are on the same page as we are. These days give us the best of our friends and confidante. They intrude in our personal space, our lives and eventually become an indispensable part of our lives. We also learn about people and the conjuring hidden behind convincing gestures. We start to live our lives with full freedom and liberty. But with freedom, comes responsibilities. We tend to forget those and end up messing our lives. The time is solely under our own control and we need to utilize it with our brains. Also college environment and hostel life, in a combined way, give us a wholesome simulation of the life that waits for us ahead. There is absolutely no one to cherish our happy moments with us, and to share our sorrow.

Hostel life makes us all rounder in terms of all types of work. Right from waking up to sleeping, from maintaining ourselves, to taking care of our belongings. With tedium and patience by our side, we eventually learn these things and become self dependent.. Also our true self and character comes out and we are able to understand ourselves better. It seems quite paranoid to make new friends and acquaintances, specially when we are from different backgrounds and have different opinions over matters. But what matters the most is how we feel about each other and if that bond is strong, people are able to forget their differences and we find an ocean of brotherhood and warmth, which we are usually searching for. There is another aspect of hostels, our seniors. Seniors are just like our mentors, who constantly pretend to be quite dangerous and over-imposing. But they prove to be our best aides as time passes. Moreover, they are a constant source of knowledge and experiences, as they have faced all those situations in their lives also. the same implies to the authorities which seem to be quite intruding and nosey over our matters. But eventually we learn that all strictness and scolding are actually meant to keep us in track. The most fearful moment of a fresher's life is an unwanted rendezvous with seniors, especially in their rooms. It is popularly known as an introduction session and one can not deny the importance these sessions exercise in our personality development later on. These days teach us the way of living, of making friends, of living the life to its fullest and stripe- off our agonies and arrogance. It just like getting a new big family and life altogether.

Thus I can definitely say that this can be the best or the worst chapter of our lives, depending on how we perceive it altogether. The sole fact that is not going to change is that we have to live here and get all the best that we can for ourselves, out of it. The faster we understand this fact, the better it is for us. It was the best I could write to share my feelings about hostel life. With all its best and worst sides and aspects, this is our hostel life, the avenue for our future destination, and home to a lot of memories!!
